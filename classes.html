Essay Writing

So many people get lost in the idea of an essay, totally bogged down in the idea that they can't write, and if they can, well, there's just NO way to predict what a teacher or prof wants. So how on earth can they do it consistently well?

This free lesson gives you a basic formula to follow that gets you a great piece of academic writing, no matter the requirements or topic. It basically comes down to:

  • Organization
  • Intention
  • Language
  • Details/Simplicity
